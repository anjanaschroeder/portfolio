Anjana is an experienced and award-winning television producer.
She is an industry and community leader with a proven track record of creating and implementing successful newscasts and programs.
Anjana embraces evolving multi-platform technologies in order to successfully market to a variety of audiences, and has a working knowledge of mainstream and emerging social media channels.

Journalists who inspire and challenge her include Ann Curry, Oprah, Diane Sawyer, Barbara Walters, Peter Jennings, Tom Brokaw, Bill Bonds, and Scott Pelley.

"Journalists are not activists, nor are they sitting on the sidelines. They are in the fray, empowering humanity." -Ann Curry

Anjana has a high priority for mentorship and personal and professional development. She is always looking to improve and expand her skills and industry knowledge.
Throughout her career in journalism Anjana has had a variety of newsroom jobs in television, radio, and digital platforms.
Her work experience in the last six years includes:

  1. Field Producer for WXYZ-TV
  2. News Producer for WXYZ-TV
  3. Multimedia Producer for Crain's Detroit Business
  4. News Producer for WLNS-TV
  5. News Producer for Focal Point
  6. Correspondent for Capital News Service
  7. Reporter/Producer for Impact 89FM
